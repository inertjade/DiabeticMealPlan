ðŸ’¡ Important Notes

Day-old Rice Preparation:
Cook white rice normally, let it cool completely, then refrigerate overnight. This creates resistant starch that has 10-15% lower glycemic impact.
Portion Control is Critical:
All portions are specified to help maintain steady blood sugar levels. Stick to these measurements for best results.
Monitor Individual Responses:
Track blood glucose levels to see how your body responds to different foods and adjust accordingly.
Stay Hydrated:
Drink water between meals, not during meals to avoid diluting digestive enzymes.
Consult Your Healthcare Provider:
Before starting any new meal plan, especially if you're on diabetes medication.
